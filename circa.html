  • Circa

    Conceptual | Campaign

    Motion Graphics, Single Page, HTML5 Video

    As a conceptual project to create a digital campaign, I chose the famous sneaker brand Circa. To begin, I developed a brief and researched the brand & its audience. This led to the creation of a single-page website with two full-background videos via HTML5, meant to be shared on social media. Archived here. (2012)

  • Overview

    As a designer, I accomplished many firsts as the project went on. Working with HTML5 video proved the most difficult, since in '12 it was still relatively new. However, the deliverables were finally met, with most screen sizes targeted, except for mobile devices.

    Additional features of the deliverables:

    • Responsive to PCs & tablets (landscape)
    • Form validation & entry storage
    • Fullscreen capability for most mobile & all PC browsers
    • Unsupported screen sizes were asked to view the page on a larger screen
    • Videos' particle animation linked to their soundtracks
